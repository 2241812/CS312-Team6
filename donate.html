Child-Focused Charities

Explore organizations working tirelessly to protect, nurture, and empower children in the Philippines and beyond.

  • UNICEF Philippines

    Advocates for children’s rights, education, and protection through partnerships with local communities and government.

  • ChildHope Philippines

    Supports street children through education, healthcare, and psychosocial services to give them hope for a better future.

  • Save the Children Philippines

    Provides long-term solutions in health, education, and child protection to improve the lives of children across the country.

  • World Vision Philippines

    A Christian humanitarian organization helping vulnerable children and communities overcome poverty and injustice.

  • Cradle of Joy

    Provides shelter and care to abandoned and neglected children while helping them find loving foster families.
