Philippine Institutions for Child Protection

Learn more about organizations working to prevent child abuse and promote child welfare in the Philippines.

  • Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD)

    The government agency responsible for social protection and promoting the rights of children across the Philippines.

  • Council for the Welfare of Children (CWC)

    Leads policy-making and programs for children’s rights, survival, protection, and development.

  • Philippine Commission on Women (PCW)

    Works on women and children’s protection, gender equality, and policies against abuse and exploitation.

  • UNICEF Philippines

    Partners with the Philippine government and local communities to protect children’s rights and end child abuse.

  • ChildHope Philippines

    A non-governmental organization helping street children through education, protection, and psychosocial support.

About Safe Child Space
