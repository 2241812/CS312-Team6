Provinces We Serve

Safe Child Space extends support to children across Northern Luzon. Here are glimpses from each province we’ve reached.

Benguet

Over 5,120+ children supported. This is where Safe Child Space began its journey.

Abra

4,096+ children reached. Focused on education and child rights advocacy.

La Union

2,048+ children supported. Programs expanding through local partnerships.

Pangasinan

1,024+ children reached. Initiatives supporting coastal families.

Aurora

8,192+ children helped. Focused on reaching isolated and vulnerable areas.
