Provinces We Serve

Rights of the Future extends support to children across Northern Luzon. Each province we reach represents thousands of lives uplifted through safety, education, and empowerment.

Benguet

Over 5,120+ children supported. This is where Rights of the Future began its journey in Baguio City, now serving as the core hub for outreach.

Abra

4,096+ children reached. Our programs focus on child rights advocacy, safe learning spaces, and resilience after calamities.

La Union

2,048+ children supported. Partnerships with local groups expand reach to rural barangays, bringing education closer to communities.

Pangasinan

1,024+ children reached. Initiatives focus on coastal families, combining livelihood aid with child protection awareness.

Aurora

8,192+ children helped. Known for its remote areas, Aurora is a key focus for reaching isolated and vulnerable children.

About Rights of the Future

Rights of the Future is more than an initiative — it’s a commitment to ensuring every child feels safe, valued, and supported. Together, we build communities where children can flourish without fear and grow with dignity. From disaster relief to education campaigns, our efforts are fueled by students, volunteers, and donors who believe in change.
